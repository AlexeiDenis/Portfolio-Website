About me

My name is Alexei Denis. I graduated Bucharest University of Economics Studies with the specialization Account and Computer Science.

The first interaction with front-end was in my last year of high school when the computer science teacher introduced us to HTML. It was fascinating how a few lines of code can have an immediate impact on the page. Thus, for the certificate in information technology, I made a website with places to visit in Romania . I loved so much working on it, but at the moment I thought this work did not exist in the real world. If you are not a good mathematician and do not know to make algorithms you will not have a place in the IT world. So I took a different road. At the University, I had some programming classes and waked up my desire to make websites.

After graduating from University I took a "gap year" for only one month or two, then I learned by myself from courses and online resources.
