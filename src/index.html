About me

My name is Alexei Denis. I graduated Bucharest University of Economics Studies with the specialization Accounting and Computer Science.

The first interaction with front-end development was in my last year of high school when the computer science teacher introduced us to HTML. It was fascinating how a few lines of code can have an immediate impact on the page. Thus, for the certificate in information technology, I made a website that shows places to visit in Romania. Also, this was my first website that I've made . I loved working on it so much , but at the moment I thought this work did not exist in the real world. If you are not a good mathematician and if you don't know algorithms you will not have a place in the IT world. So I took a different road.

At the University, I had some programming classes and woke up my desire to make websites.

After graduating from University I took a break for a month or two, then I learned by myself from courses and online resources.
